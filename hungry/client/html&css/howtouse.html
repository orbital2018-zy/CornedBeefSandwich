Hungry?

How to use Hungry?


To search for recipes

At the homepage for Hungry?, type in the ingredients you have on hand into the search bar. Press search.
Hungry? will display the dishes with the specified ingredients.
Select the dish you are interested in to view the full recipe.

To look for recipes relevant to a festive season

Click on the Festive recipes button on the home page and select the
specific festival you are preparing food for. Then, search for recipes
with specific ingredientsor you could just leave it blank to show the whole
catalogue of dishes.

To create your shopping list

Click on the My Shopping List button on the home page. For now,
key in the ingredients that you would be interested in buying for your
next shopping trip. It can't be save yet, so just keep the browser open,
preferable on your phone. We would try to add in the additional functions
by milestone 3.

To suggest a new recipe

Click on the Suggest new recipes link at the bottom of the home page.
Key in the relevant details, such as the dish name and other required fields.

To return to the home page from any other page

Simply click the phrase Hungry? in the header of the page.
