About Hungry?


Hungry? is a web application that allows users to search for specific recipes through ingredients.
It has easy-to follow instructions that allow even beginners to cook their meals.
Besides that, users could search recipes with specific themes,
such as Valentine or Halloween to prepare a festive meal for their loved ones.
With that, the shopping list notes down the ingredients required to gather
which would be convenient when preparing the ingredients.
